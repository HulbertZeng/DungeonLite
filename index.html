Welcome to the Dungeon!

In this single-player game, you play as a powerful wizard trapped in a mysterious dungeon. Your goal is to survive and defeat the waves of Demons at each dungeon level, using magic spells and upgrade items. The game ends when all dungeon levels have been completed or if you lose all your health.

Click to Play Now

How to Play

    Move: Use the W, A, S, and D keys to move around in the dungeon

    Shoot: Use your cursor to aim and click to shoot an energy ball

    Demons will follow you around and deal damage on contact
    Watch for Demons with special abilities

    Pause: Press the space key

Key Items

    Health Boost: Restores lost health

    Speed Boost: Increases movement speed

    Spell Boost: Reduces energy ball reload time
